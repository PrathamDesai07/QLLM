# README: Roadmap for LLM‑Guided Pauli‑Correlation Encoding (Direction 1)

## 0. High‑Level Goal

1. Learns and summarizes **interpretable structural patterns** in the QAOA / Pauli‑correlation design space (graph families, correlation order \(k\), Pauli assignments, trainability and approximation quality).  
2. Uses these patterns to **select \(k\)**, allocate Pauli strings, and **shape the QAOA ansatz** for qubit‑efficient optimization on NISQ hardware via Pauli‑correlation encoding.
